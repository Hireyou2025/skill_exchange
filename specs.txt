================================================================================
          AI INTERVIEW MONITORING SYSTEM: EXPLAINABILITY LAYER SPECS
================================================================================

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
The Explainability Layer transforms raw tracking logs (gaze, head pose, blinks, 
speech energy) into transparent, recruiter-grade behavioral intelligence. Every 
scoring decision, integrity flag, and behavioral event is traceable, 
evidence-backed, and qualified by a dynamic Confidence of Inference score.

2. SYSTEM COMPONENTS
--------------------
The explainability system is implemented across three core files:
- `scoring_trace.py`: Decomposes evaluation scores into positive and negative 
  impact factors.
- `event_reasoner.py`: Estimates inference confidence based on environmental noise 
  and correlates behavioral signals to construct causal explanation chains.
- `explanation_engine.py`: Aggregates frame-by-frame stats, determines metric 
  evidence, and generates recruiter-readable summaries.

3. EXPLAINABLE SCORING & MATHEMATICAL TRACEBACK
-----------------------------------------------
The final Session Score is calculated out of 100% and decomposed into five 
underlying factors. The sum of these factors matches the final score exactly.

Score Formula:
  Session Score = (Attention + Head Pose + Comm) - (Gaze Dev + Stress + Integrity Penalty)

Where:
- Attention Consistency (Max +40): Proportional to centered gaze percentage 
  (session_attention * 40).
- Stable Head Pose (Max +20): Proportional to centered posture percentage 
  (session_focus * 20).
- Strong Communication (Max +40): Proportional to vocal responsiveness and 
  expressed confidence (session_confidence * 40).
- Frequent Gaze Deviation (Max -10): Penalty based on off-screen gaze ratio.
- Elevated Stress (Max -20): Penalty based on rolling stress average.
- Integrity Penalty: Computed dynamically to scale down the final score 
  proportionally (Base Score * (1 - Integrity Score / 100)).

4. DYNAMIC CONFIDENCE OF INFERENCE
----------------------------------
To avoid false positives under poor capture conditions, the system calculates 
a dynamic confidence coefficient (0.0 to 1.0) and applies specific deductions:
- Low Lighting (-0.15): Triggered if average frame brightness < 55.
- Motion Blur (-0.15): Triggered if Laplacian variance of the frame < 90.
- Unstable Landmarks (-0.20): Triggered if nose tip position jitter > 6.0px.
- Partial Face Visibility (-0.20): Triggered if face mesh is lost or flickering.
- ROI Instability (-0.10): Triggered if face width size variance > 8%.

5. CAUSAL EVENT REASONING & CLUSTERING
--------------------------------------
When a behavioral threshold is crossed, the Event Reasoner inspects the rolling 
state window to provide a causal chain explaining *why* the event occurred:
- Attention Drop: Caused by off-screen gaze, head-down posture, or speech inactivity.
- Stress Spike: Caused by increased blink frequency, speech hesitation (silence), 
  or gaze instability.
- Communication Confidence Improved: Caused by sustained verbal response or 
  centered gaze alignment.
- Integrity Warning: Caused by candidate absent from ROI or multiple faces detected.

6. LEGAL & ETHICAL SAFETY BOUNDARIES
------------------------------------
To maintain legal compliance and prevent biased psychological labeling:
- Avoid absolute declarations (e.g. "Candidate is lying / depressed / anxious").
- Use descriptive behavioral terminology instead:
  - "Elevated stress indicators detected"
  - "Attention instability observed"
  - "Behavioral confidence decreased temporarily"

7. VISUALIZATIONS & REPORTING
-----------------------------
- Recruiter Dashboard: Renders live metrics, visual quality levels, current 
  inference confidence, and scoring traceback contributors.
- Graphical Session Report: Plots attention/focus scores with a shaded error-band 
  representing the confidence interval, flags chronological events with causal 
  chains, and prints a final recruiter insights summary.
- Markdown Session Report: Automatically writes a structured, human-readable 
  report (session_report.md) for post-interview review.
